My name is Andrew, and I’m currently employed as an interaction designer at Regions Bank where I have the privilege of working remotely from my home office near Atlanta. My responsibilities include leading design projects and managing work efforts for an interdisciplinary team. I also do graphic, visual, and motion design work as well as front end web development on the side.

I studied industrial design at Georgia Tech and typography at Savannah College of Art and Design while gaining real-world work experience at CNN and Turner Broadcasting. Since then I’ve completed a variety of projects for prominent financial institutions, local small businesses, non-profits, and friends.

I’ve created everything from company logos and WordPress websites to wedding invitations and wooden cabinets, and in my free time I enjoy similarly creative activities like playing guitar, restoring old furniture, taking photographs, and traveling around the country. View my portfolio and resume to learn more about my work and contact me if you’d like to talk—I’m always interested in making
new connections!
